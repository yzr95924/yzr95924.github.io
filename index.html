About me

Zuoru Yang got his Bachelor's degree from Department of Computer Science and Technology, Xi'an Jiaotong University in 2018. In XJTU, he worked with Prof Peng Zhang to do the research about Software-Defined Network (SDN) and Network Function Virtualization (NFV) (e.g. design debug/verification tool in SDN and NFV). Now, he is pursuing his PhD degree in ADSLab (Applied Distributed System Lab) of The Chinese University of Hong Kong under the supervision by Prof Patrick P. C. Lee.
His research interests span Network Security and Distributed Storage System. Currently, he focuses on the topic of Secure Deduplication in cloud storage system.
